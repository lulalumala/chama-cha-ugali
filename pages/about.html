ABOUT US

Chama Cha Ugali is a group of same age group that has been existing for over ten years. It was formed by liberal minded individuals within Kibera whose unifying factor was their immense appetite and love for the tasteless lump of starch, UGALI.

MISSION

Whilst having their regular foodbowl and other events, Chama Cha Ugali seeks to impact positive energy and attitude to the upcoming generations. This will in turn give them a positive approach to various fields of life.

VISION

  • Giving advice to younger generations
  • Recruiting talents to the team
  • Looking for better ways the younger generations can generate income
  • Giving alternative and unbiased views to the society
